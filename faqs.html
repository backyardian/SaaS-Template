What is Grammarly?

Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant designed to help users write clearly, correctly, and confidently. It checks grammar, spelling, punctuation, and style while providing suggestions to improve overall writing quality.

How does Grammarly work?

Grammarly analyzes your text as you type and offers real-time suggestions. It identifies grammatical mistakes, spelling errors, awkward phrasing, and other writing issues, helping you improve your content instantly.

Is Grammarly free?

Yes. Grammarly offers a free plan that includes essential grammar, spelling, and punctuation checks. Users can also upgrade to Premium for advanced writing suggestions and additional features.

What features are included in Grammarly Premium?

Grammarly Premium provides advanced grammar checks, vocabulary enhancement, tone detection, clarity improvements, plagiarism detection, and AI-powered writing assistance.

Can Grammarly detect plagiarism?

Yes. Grammarly Premium includes a plagiarism checker that scans your text against billions of web pages and academic resources to help ensure originality.

Where can I use Grammarly?

Grammarly works across multiple platforms, including web browsers, Microsoft Word, Google Docs, desktop applications, and mobile devices. This allows you to receive writing assistance wherever you write.

Is my personal information secure?

Grammarly uses industry-standard security practices and encryption to protect user data. Privacy and security are a top priority for the platform.

Does Grammarly support languages other than English?

Grammarly primarily supports English and several English dialects, including American, British, Canadian, and Australian English.

Can students use Grammarly?

Absolutely. Grammarly is widely used by students to improve essays, research papers, assignments, and other academic writing while learning better writing habits.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy. Create an account, install Grammarly on your preferred device or browser, and begin writing. Grammarly will automatically provide suggestions to help improve your text.
