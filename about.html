Our Beginning

Founded to improve communication.

Grammarly was founded in 2009 by Max Lytvyn, Alex Shevchenko, and Dmytro Lider. The founders believed that technology could help people express their ideas more clearly and confidently.


What started as a grammar and spelling checker quickly evolved into a sophisticated AI-powered communication assistant used by millions around the world.
